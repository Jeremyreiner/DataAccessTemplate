Publify

Empowering the next

generation of educators

Publify is an easy way for educators to share course material with their students.

  • Take control over the content, design, and distribution of your work.

  • Make education more accessible for students of all financial backgrounds.

  • Receive higher payments in royalties & reduce cost for students by up to 90%.

Our mission

We aim to empower professors to self-publish their research and teaching materials, enabling them to share their knowledge with a global audience and make a meaningful impact in their field. We believe that by enabling professors to share their expertise and insights, we can help advance education and scholarship around the world.
